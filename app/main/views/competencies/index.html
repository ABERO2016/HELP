Leadership Competencies

Self Awareness

Exhibits knowledge of personal values, strengths, shortcomings, and developmental opportunities. Uses self-assessment strategies to inform personal growth and development.

Learn more: Traits of a Self Aware Leader

Intentional Learner

Has knowledge of personal learning style and leverages this knowledge to enhance personal performance, knowledge of self, others, and leadership ability.

Learn more: Traits of an Intentional Learner

Communicates Effectively

Has the ability to communicate effectively through a variety of methods, media, and within a range of contexts.

Learn more: Traits of an Effective Communicator

Develops Relationships

Creates a positive, welcoming environment; able to see issues from multiple points of view and builds effective inter and intra-group relationships.

Learn more: Traits of a Strong Relationship Builder

Diversity and Differences

Recognizes the value of different perspectives, skillskets, and people; is able to facilitate productive outcomes in diverse groups.

Learn more: Traits of an Inclusive Collaborator

Engaging Leadership

Enables others to step up and lead; creates conditions for others to shine; generates collective commitment and learning.

Learn more: Traits of an Engaging Leader

Directive Leadership

Coordinates members and maintains accountability for effective outcomes. Sets direction, communicates expectations, and monitors progress.

Learn more: Traits of a Directive Leader

Champions Effective Processes

Establishes systems and processes that facilitate efficient and effective outcomes.

Problem Solving

Facilititates effective problem identification and solving strategies; identifies and cultivates resources; and delivers results.

Learn more: Traits of a Strategic Problem Solver

Strategic Perspective

Able to evaluate issues from multiple perspectives and identify the core problem; considers options and trade-offs; makes effective use of resources.

Learn more: Traits of an Open Minded Leader

Ethics and Integrity

Knowledgeable of and committed to a meaningful set of ethical guidelines and principles. Actions are consistent with personal values.

Learn more: Traits of an Ethical Leader

Innovative Spirit

Displays the courage to initiate positive chage; innovative; will risk failing in front of peers.

Learn more: Traits of an Innovative Leader
