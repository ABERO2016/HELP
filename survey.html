Hart Leadership Assessment 2016

As a student in the Booby B. Lyle School of Engineering, you will receive a world-class education in engineering and computer science. You will learn technical theory, technical skills, as well as necessary teamwork and leadership skills needed to apply this knowledge. The Hart Leadership Assessment (HLA) will help you identify your leadership strengths and growth areas. It should take approximately 20-25 minutes minutes to complete. On August 19, after you have settled in to your residence hall, the Hart Center for Engineering Leadership will distribute your personalized report based on your responses. Equipped with these results, and with the ongoing assistance of the Hart Center for Engineering Leadership, we encourage you to find meaningful ways to engage at SMU.
